A story ended early It’s weird. The moment I received the email, I was across the border as a tourist in our Northern neighbor. I was supposed to be across the waters as a tourist in our Western neighbors. My friend and I had to cancel our Senior year Spring Break trip to Europe last minute due to Coronavirus. Amidst all the chaos going on in Europe, we did not have any inclination that America would be the next victim. That’s why driving by the Niagara Falls and recieving the email by Jim Ryan, University of Virginia President, that school would be cancelled was exhilirating. That was my initial emotion. I felt released from school, released from the responsibility that came with it.
